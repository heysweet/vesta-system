Dive bar where the party meets. [[Kuhl Simple]] left a note for [[Staub Sternenlicht]] saying only "Meet the ribbit at Peggy's Third Leg." The bartender is Brennec, a burly male dwarf. Brennec and [[Darius Sunseeker]] know each other. 

The wall is littered with tattered posters: Solaris "The Dome Saves". A military recruiting poster. PI services poster. An ad for the upcoming "Battle of the Beasts" battle of the bands.
