Warden of Renewal | Druid

Twenty-Two was designed by the ancient and lost people of [[Y'shika]] - something happened to her long ago that knocked her out for centuries. When she arose, her planet had changed and the people gone. Completely abandoned on the planet and only glimpses into her lost world, she developed an attunement for nature and the stars. She walked her planet alone for centuries before someone found her and brought her into the current state of the world. She is known by some as [[Twenty-Two|the Horned One]], and may be pivotal in unlocking memories that have to do with the creation of the [[Interplanetary Portals]].
