Twenty-Two was designed by the ancient and lost people of [[Y'shika]] - something happened to her long ago that knocked her out for centuries. When she arose, her planet had changed and the people gone. Completely abandoned on the planet and only glimpses into her lost world, she developed an attunement for nature and the stars. She walked her planet alone for centuries before someone found her and brought her into the current state of the world. She is known by some as [[Twenty-Two|the Horned One]], and may be pivotal in unlocking memories that have to do with the creation of the [[Interplanetary Portals]].

# Story So Far

As Twenty-Two awakens, they see visions: a rare flower bloom, the perpetual glow of twilight, the smell of burning, a huge pink glowing portal, a glad full of life, a crater.

"M'lady, we must get out of here!" Standing in front of her is an odd sight, a child-sized turtle-clank knight in shining armor named [[Vector]]. Twenty-Two is ages out of time, disoriented, and cautious about her unknown savior. As their scrapper captors come back, Twenty-Two transforms into a wolf and takes them down. With Vector on her back, Twenty-Two drags the dead elf through the barrier to bypass the lifeform detector.

They run into an odd band of allies during their escape -- the trenchcoat-clad ribbit detective [[Gork Boulderbouncer|Gork]], the bumbling brawns of the operation [[Darius Sunseeker|Darius]], and the oracle faerie [[Staub Sternenlicht|Staub]] whose eyes excitedly go wide when they realize they are face-to-face with the golden horned one.
