A passage from Darius:

*I have long thought of the days gone by, the simple exaltations of finding a place to sleep or a bite to eat. In my orphaned youth, those two things were hard to come by most days. As I grew up, I used my natural brawn for odd jobs and the like to get by, hauling heavy cargo from the merchant ships and tradesman carriages paid a fair wage for the menial labor that it was. I applied to be one of the city guard but was denied as I didn’t have any reputable references to give, but I found my calling when an innocent little girl’s cat was stuck in a tree. I climbed the tree and fetched the cat for her, sustaining multiple wounds from both the thorns of the tree and the claws of the cat. The little girl was so thankful that she gave me the uneaten half of her sweetroll. I knew then and there that helping people for the purest sake of helping people made me feel fulfilled and gave me the purpose I was seeking. Gracious people pay graciously when they can, and I did not expect a handout for every good deed. When a monitory or culinary thank you did come my way, I humbly accepted each offer. During one of my patrols, I noticed another do-gooder abouts, an amphibian of some renown. I only knew of his reputation and did not know his name. We exchanged pleasantries in passing, and went on our way.* 

*Usually working as a lone wolf, the fleeting thought of adding [[Gork Boulderbouncer|this Ribbit]] to my chosen family quickly escaped my memory, which was usually a steel trap. Time will tell if we will meet again as kindred spirits fighting against the injustices of this world, or as mortal foes combatting against our conflicting ideologies. In the meantime, I’m hungry and require snacks.*
