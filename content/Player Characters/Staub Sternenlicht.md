Staub is a Faerie Beastkin born on Terra, the sister planet known for her dense jungles, heavy gravity, and vibrant life. Fae are native to Terra and deeply tied to the Bells Tribe, who oversee mail delivery, trade routes, and neutral territories.  
  
When the Cataclysm struck Vesta, the Fae Tribes fled through the Interplanetary Portals to Terra, Staub’s family was among them, leaving behind ancestral routes, alliances, and sacred groves. 

But when the portals destabilized in the aftermath, Staub became trapped on Vesta, cut off from Terra, and the rest of the Bells. 

This exile shapes his worldview. 

He is a courier without a route, a messenger with no home. 
  
Within the Bells, Staub held a rare role: Courier of Fortunes, a diviner who delivers predictions, warnings, and blessings alongside physical messages. In Fae culture, a fortune is a kind of parcel: wrapped, carried, and delivered with intention.  
  
Staub is not formally aligned with any major faction, his relationships are shaped by the man who took care of Staub during his years of blindness, [[Kuhl Simple]]. Staub is respected by [[Duologists]] for his devotion to Attis the All-Mother. He is distrusted by [[anarchists]], omens are dangerous to those who live by “might makes right”  
  
He is politically neutral by necessity, but spiritually and ethically aligned with balance, survival, and the fragile hope Attis represents.  
  
Staub was born under a single-shadowed eclipse of [[Two Suns|Attis]], a rare sunclipse where only the All-Mother’s light remained. Faeries believe such births mark children touched by fate. His mentor, taught him that Fate is a puzzle. Predicting the outcome is part of playing the game.  
Kuhl believed Staub’s visions were connected to the ancient Infernis Portals, and that understanding their magic would bring him closer to Attis.
  
Satub’s magic is cast through runes, the same symbols that appear on the frames of the Interplanetary Portals. These runes also cover his arms and fingers, glowing faintly when he reads the strands of time.
  
Staub is an Umbral Seer, channeling the shadowed possibilities of fate. When he peers into the future, Umbral energy vibrates from his fingertips, revealing the darker paths others avoid.  
  
Staub is quiet, eerie, mathematical. He does not force fate, he is an observer. And a messenger.  
  
Personal Stakes:
  
Staub understands how the [[Humes|Hume]] colonization efforts displaced Beastkin tribes, including the Fae. He carries memories of broken treaties, stolen routes, sacred lands repurposed for [[Terradome|Terradomes]]. This injustice fuels his desire to protect neutral territories and maintain balance.
  
Guided by the teachings of his mentor Kuhl Simple, Staub seeks to understand the magic that binds the planets, the gods, and the strands of Fate. His visions are both gift and burden. Shaped by past failures to save lives, he is determined to choose the best path. He seeks [[Twenty-Two|the Horned One]], a rumored home of all knowledge. To free him from the visions of sorrow, and bring him into enlightenment with [[Nine Gods|Attis]], the All-Mother.
